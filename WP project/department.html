Our Departments

Explore the diverse academic disciplines we offer

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

The CSE department is the hub of innovation and research, preparing students in AI, ML, cybersecurity, and more. It provides industry-aligned curriculum, world-class labs, and international collaborations.

Information Technology (IT)

The IT department blends software engineering, networks, data science, and cloud computing. With modern infrastructure and industry training, it equips students for global careers in IT and software fields.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

ECE at GNITC focuses on embedded systems, IoT, VLSI, and communication networks. Students gain both theoretical and practical exposure through lab sessions and hands-on projects.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical department imparts knowledge in thermodynamics, robotics, manufacturing, and CAD/CAM. It emphasizes innovation, practical design, and research for modern engineering solutions.
